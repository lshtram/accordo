# accordo-gradient
## Vibrant Gradient Presentations

*Six built-in color variants*

---

# Default Gradient

This is the default gradient — purple to violet, goes great with white text and gold accents.

- Bold headings with text-shadow for depth
- Translucent code blocks (`glass` effect)
- Gold-colored inline code and links

> Blockquotes use a frosted-glass treatment.

---

<!-- _class: ocean -->

# Ocean Variant

`<!-- _class: ocean -->`

Deep navy to teal. Authoritative yet calm.

- Technical discussions
- Data-heavy slides
- Building trust in numbers

---

<!-- _class: sunset -->

# Sunset Variant

`<!-- _class: sunset -->`

Pink → red → orange. High energy, memorable, emotional.

Best for:
- Product launches
- Big announcements
- Closing slides

---

<!-- _class: forest -->

# Forest Variant

`<!-- _class: forest -->`

Green gradient. Fresh, natural, growth-oriented.

Use for:
- Sustainability topics
- Growth metrics
- Healthy systems messaging

---

<!-- _class: midnight -->

# Midnight Variant

`<!-- _class: midnight -->`

Dark blue to lighter blue. Classic, trustworthy, serious.

The most "professional" gradient option — works in conservative contexts.

---

<!-- _class: emerald -->

# Emerald Variant

`<!-- _class: emerald -->`

Teal to green. Fresh and modern.

Great for:
- Health/wellness topics
- Success metrics
- Environment-related content

---

<!-- _class: aurora -->

# Aurora Variant

`<!-- _class: aurora -->`

Very dark atmospheric blue.
Like looking up at a night sky.

Dramatic. Contemplative. Unique.

---

## Use different variants for different sections

<!-- _class: lead -->

# One More Tip

The `lead` class on `accordo-gradient` always gives you the dark night-sky look — regardless of which variant your slide uses. Perfect for title slides.
